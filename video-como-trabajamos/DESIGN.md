## Style Prompt
Editorial brand film for creativv Studio — a Venezuelan design+code+AI studio. Soft daylight, tropical modernism on a cream canvas. Confident, minimal, slow. Each beat is one declaration. Greens of the Ávila against papyrus paper. Movement is patient, type is the hero.

## Colors
- `#e8e7df` — papiro (primary bg, cream)
- `#336443` — verde-ávila (primary ink, dark green)
- `#85AB8B` — verde-musgo (secondary accent, sage)
- `#1f2a1d` — ink (deep green-black for high-contrast type)
- `#3d5638` — verde-medio (rule lines, secondary copy)
- `#c4d2bf` — verde-leve (atmospheric wash, decorative)
- `#d8d6c8` — papiro-soft (panel tint)

## Typography
- Fraunces — display, serif, italic emphasis on key words
- Inter — sans, metadata, labels, body

## What NOT to Do
- No pure white (#fff) backgrounds
- No blue/purple tech-startup gradients
- No fast bounces, springs, or playful elastics
- No centered metadata — flush left/right or editorial grid
- No solid flat color blocks — every scene needs texture or wash
- No emoji or icon decorations beyond hairline rules
