## Style Prompt
Editorial night-city video for a Venezuelan software studio. Cinematic darkness, ink and moonlight. Slow-burn reveals, typeface-forward. Words appear as declarations. Warm amber against deep navy — tropical modernism.

## Colors
- `#0a1f2e` — noche (primary bg)
- `#06151f` — noche-deep (transition overlay)
- `#f0ead6` — papiro (primary text, scene 2 bg)
- `#2a6ea0` — cobalto (accent, italic emphasis)
- `#d4cdb8` — papiro-soft (muted metadata)
- `#c89464` — guayoyo (warm rule lines)

## Typography
- Fraunces — display, serif, expressive headlines
- JetBrains Mono — metadata, labels, captions

## What NOT to Do
- No bright white (#fff) anywhere
- No san-serif body copy
- No fast motion, bounces, or playful springs
- No blue-purple tech-startup gradients
- No centered metadata (always flush left/right or editorial grid)
