# 5-client confidence plan

## Reality

100% certainty impossible before market response. Best controllable path: enough qualified conversations to make 5 closes statistically plausible.

## Required pipeline

Assumptions:
- 8% reply from personalized outbound
- 45% reply -> sales conversation
- 35% conversation -> paid diagnostic
- 35% diagnostic -> pilot/sprint

To close 5 clients:
- 160 personalized prospects
- 13 replies
- 6 conversations
- 2 diagnostics/pilots minimum from cold

Because warm/referral converts higher, target:
- 80 warm/local/referral
- 120 cold personalized
- 20 partner/referrer asks

## Non-negotiable assets

- homepage with offer ladder
- 4 vertical pages
- `/cotizar` with clear plans
- diagnostic template
- scripts
- CRM sheet
- Loom demo
- 5 short audits

## Daily operating target

For 10 business days:
- 20 new prospects researched
- 15 personalized messages sent
- 5 follow-ups
- 1 short audit video
- 1 LinkedIn post

Total:
- 200 prospects
- 150 outbound messages
- 50 follow-ups
- 10 audit videos
- 10 posts

## Close path

Step 1: free 2-idea tease.

Step 2: USD 150 diagnostic.

Step 3: USD 900+ pilot or USD 1.500+ sprint.

Step 4: testimonial + case study after first measurable win.

## Stop conditions

If reply rate under 4% after 50 messages:
- make message shorter
- lead with Loom/audit
- target warmer prospects

If replies but no diagnostics:
- price objection -> offer USD 100 founder batch for first 5
- trust objection -> show project screenshots + refund/credit policy
- unclear ROI -> sell one small pilot, not full project

If diagnostics but no projects:
- improve close doc with exact scope, metric, timeline, 50/50 terms

## Founder batch

Use scarcity honestly:

> Opening 5 founder diagnostics this week. I want case studies, so I am doing deeper teardown + roadmap for USD 150 and crediting it toward build.

## Minimum confidence threshold

Do not claim confidence until:
- 100 prospects researched
- 60 personalized messages sent
- 5 replies
- 2 live calls or serious WhatsApp threads
- 1 diagnostic paid or verbally accepted

After threshold, keep pushing until:
- 200 prospects
- 10 serious conversations
- 3 diagnostics/pilots
- 1 signed build
